**Period:** October 1 -- December 31, 2024
**Approved By:** Executive Leadership Team

Q4 is our largest revenue quarter and a critical period for setting up FY2025 momentum. We are prioritizing enterprise deal closure, platform reliability for peak season, and headcount ramp to support H1 2025 product launches.

## Scoring Methodology

OKRs are scored 0.0--1.0: **0.0--0.3** = significant miss (requires retrospective), **0.4--0.6** = partial progress (carry forward if relevant), **0.7--1.0** = strong achievement (target range). Scores reviewed in Q4 retrospective (January 2025).
